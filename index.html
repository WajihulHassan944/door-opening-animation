Projects of HoPe zakładka



1. We will vote on exhibitions’ artists, topics, locations and contribute to design and entourage, so that we set our mark properly before we have our own museum space.



2. Some of You will be given chance to create Your own exhibition room, so that we can test that feature before introducing it as a permanent element for community when the museum will open



3. We will regularly meet on HoPe extravaganzas as well as organise and visit photography-related events. Let’s be mainstream and do yacht first.



4. We want to diversify sales of photographs and sell them like high end fashion. We will ask You for advice, so that those with diamond eyes for photography will be properly rewarded.



5. Can creative photos overcome selfies ?



6. Mexicans celebrate Dia de Los Muertos, Argentina wins the world cup title, top brand organises a costume party ? We want to be there and take You there, to create photos which will not die



7. We will give community members a free hand to propose us initiatives for collaborations with branda/photography-related projects. Coolest photo in top NBA club kit wins 2 tickets for their game ? Let’s organise photography conference ? Or maybe we will create a photo-based t-shirt design for a fashion house ? We will do everything to put Your best and most deliberate initiatives into life and prove that photography is the longest axis in the world !



8. Photo Awards will be HoPe major annual competition, based on completely different rules than exisisting awards. We will invite our community members to co-create this unique projects through participation and select one community member to the jury.
